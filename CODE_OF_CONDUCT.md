# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful environment in all interactions across our public repositories.

We follow the **Contributor Covenant** Code of Conduct.

---

## Our Standards

We encourage:
- Respectful and constructive communication  
- Consideration for diverse backgrounds and perspectives  
- Collaboration and problem-solving  
- Professionalism in public discussion  

We do not tolerate:
- Harassment or discrimination  
- Disrespectful or aggressive behavior  
- Sharing sensitive or private information  
- Unprofessional conduct
