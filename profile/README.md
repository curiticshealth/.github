# Curitics Health

Building the engineering platform powering value-based care at scale.

We design and operate a multi-tenant healthcare data platform that brings together eligibility, claims, clinical data, attribution logic, and workflow engines—built with security, correctness, and operational excellence at the core.

---

## 🔒 Engineering Principles

- **Security First**  
  RLS everywhere, strict tenancy boundaries, principle of least privilege.

- **Data Integrity Over Speed**  
  Every pipeline is deterministic, auditable, and replay-safe.

- **Operational Clarity**  
  Simple deploys, predictable queues, structured monitoring.

- **Reliable by Design**  
  We value boring, stable engineering over clever complexity.

- **Developer Happiness**  
  Clear conventions, automated processes, clean tooling.
